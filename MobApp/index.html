Simple

Whatever platform, there's a sugar app for you.

Sugar is used by almost 3 million children around the world every day. It has been designed from the ground up to be as user friendly as possible. With a constantly expanding activity base, the opportunities of what sugar can do are limitless.

Education

One of our key goals at Sugar is to offer freely and readily available activites to learners everywhere.

The Sugar platform provides a wide range of educational activiies that can be used by educators across the world.

View Actvities


Sugar Labs is working with educators around the world to focus on these learning challenges:

  • To make Sugar and Sugar activities freely and readily available to learners everywhere.
  • To explore and share best practices.
  • To provide a forum for discussion and support for technology for learning.
  • To provide mechanism for evaluation and dissemination of results.

Open Source

All of our projects are completely open source. Allowing educators and software developers across the world to extend our platform for the better.
